About

What is Curiosity?

The NCERT Class 8 "Curiosity" textbook is part of the new National Curriculum Framework (NCF 2023) initiative. It integrates concepts from Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Earth Sciences into a cohesive, activity-driven science course that fosters scientific thinking and competency-based learning.

  • 🎯
    Competency Based — Questions that test understanding, not just memory.
  • 🧪
    Activity Driven — Every chapter has hands-on experiments.
  • 🌍
    Real World Connect — Science linked to everyday life and society.
  • 📖
    Integrated Approach — Blends multiple science disciplines seamlessly.
